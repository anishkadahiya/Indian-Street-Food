Shawarma

Non-Veg • Delhi

Prep Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Shawarma is a Middle Eastern dish popular in Delhi street food, made with marinated chicken wrapped in flatbread with sauces.

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Garlic sauce
  • Vegetables
  • Pita bread
  • Spices
  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Cook or grill chicken.
  3. Slice into strips.
  4. Place in bread with veggies and sauce.
  5. Wrap and serve.

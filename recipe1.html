Chole Bhature

Veg • Delhi

Prep Time: 45 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Origin

Chole Bhature is one of the most famous street foods of Delhi. It consists of spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried fluffy bread called bhature. It is loved for its rich flavors and satisfying combination.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (for bhature)
  • Curd and baking soda
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas until soft.
  2. Heat oil and add cumin seeds, onions, and sauté until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and tomato puree, cook well.
  4. Add spices and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add chickpeas and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  6. Prepare dough using flour, curd, and baking soda.
  7. Roll and deep fry bhature until puffed.
  8. Serve hot with chole and garnish with coriander.

Chicken Tikka

Non-Veg • Punjab

Prep Time: 50 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Chicken Tikka is a popular Punjabi dish made by marinating chicken pieces in spices and grilling them to perfection.

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric
  • Garam masala
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices.
  2. Keep aside for 30 minutes.
  3. Skewer the chicken pieces.
  4. Grill or cook on pan until fully cooked.
  5. Serve hot with chutney.

Pani Puri

Prep Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 20 puris
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • Mint leaves
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Mash boiled potatoes and mix with chickpeas.
  2. Add salt and chaat masala to the mixture.
  3. Prepare spicy mint water by blending mint leaves and spices.
  4. Crack a small hole in each puri.
  5. Fill puris with potato mixture.
  6. Add tamarind chutney inside.
  7. Pour mint water carefully into each puri.
  8. Serve immediately for best taste.

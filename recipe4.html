Dhokla

Veg • Ahmedabad

Prep Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Dhokla is a soft, fluffy snack from Gujarat made using fermented batter and steamed to perfection.

  • 1 cup gram flour
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Green chilies
  • Salt
  1. Prepare smooth batter.
  2. Add baking soda and mix well.
  3. Pour into greased tray and steam.
  4. Prepare tempering and pour over dhokla.
  5. Cut and serve.

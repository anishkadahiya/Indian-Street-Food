Egg Roll

Non-Veg • Kolkata

Prep Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Egg Roll is a popular street food from Kolkata. It consists of a crispy paratha layered with egg and filled with onions, sauces, and spices, rolled into a convenient wrap.

Ingredients

  • 2 parathas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • Tomato ketchup
  • Chili sauce
  • Salt and spices

Steps

  1. Cook paratha on a pan.
  2. Crack egg on top and spread evenly.
  3. Flip and cook both sides.
  4. Add onions and sauces.
  5. Roll tightly and serve hot.

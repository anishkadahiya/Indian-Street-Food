Poha Jalebi

Veg • Indore

Prep Time: 35 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Origin

Poha Jalebi is a famous breakfast combination from Indore. It perfectly balances sweet and savory flavors, making it a unique and popular street food dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flattened rice (poha)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Green chilies
  • Peanuts
  • Mustard seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Curry leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Fresh coriander
  • 1 cup maida (for jalebi)
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • Sugar syrup
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Wash poha and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, peanuts, and onions.
  3. Add turmeric and poha, mix well.
  4. Add salt, lemon juice, and garnish with coriander.
  5. Prepare jalebi batter using maida and curd, ferment for few hours.
  6. Pipe batter into hot oil in spiral shapes.
  7. Deep fry and dip into sugar syrup.
  8. Serve poha hot with crispy jalebi.

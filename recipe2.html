Vada Pav

Veg • Mumbai

Prep Time: 30 mins

Difficulty: Easy

Origin

Vada Pav is Mumbai’s most iconic street food. It consists of a spicy potato fritter placed inside a soft bread bun, served with chutneys and fried green chilies.

  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • Green chilies
  • Garlic
  • Mustard seeds
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Gram flour (besan)
  • Bread buns (pav)
  • Oil for frying
  1. Mash potatoes and mix with spices.
  2. Prepare small balls and dip in gram flour batter.
  3. Deep fry until golden brown.
  4. Slice pav and apply chutney.
  5. Place vada inside and serve hot.

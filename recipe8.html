Seekh Kebab

Non-Veg • Lucknow

Prep Time: 45 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Seekh Kebab is a famous Mughlai delicacy from Lucknow. It is made using minced meat mixed with aromatic spices, shaped onto skewers, and grilled to perfection. It is known for its juicy texture and smoky flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced meat
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander

Steps

  1. Mix minced meat with onions, spices, and herbs.
  2. Shape the mixture onto skewers.
  3. Grill or roast evenly on medium heat.
  4. Cook until browned and juicy.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney.

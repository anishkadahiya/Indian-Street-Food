Kulcha

Veg • Amritsar

Prep Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium

Kulcha is a traditional Punjabi bread from Amritsar, often stuffed with spiced potatoes and served with chole.

  • 2 cups flour
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Coriander
  • Spices
  • Butter
  1. Prepare soft dough.
  2. Prepare potato stuffing.
  3. Stuff and roll kulcha.
  4. Cook on tawa until golden.
  5. Apply butter and serve.
